Tripper

Tripper is a tool to help people plan upcoming trips. It allows them to view attractions near the location of their trip, associate those attractions with their trip, set a budget, and more.

Tripper was created using Bootstrap, Django, and PSQL by a team of three engineers: Melody Cerritos, Mert Nacakgedigi, and Elizabeth Patton.

Visit Tripper
Worldbuilder

Worldbuilder is a tool for writers, dungeon masters, and anyone looking to keep track of a world of their own creation. A world is a wide, complicated thing to keep track of, and Worldbuilder allows for easy data storage, as well as providing questions and links to aspiring creators to help keep the inspiration flowing.

Worldbuilder was built using Javascript, Django, UIkit, Python, and Javascript. It runs on a PostgreSQL server.
